Why did I go to Hoi An?

Hoi An Things

Hoi An is super close to a beautiful beach town called Da Nang. While staying in Da Nang, you have to trek over to Hoi An for a visit!

Hoi An has a well preserved old town area that's referred to as Ancient town.

There's cool sites to visit and tasty food.

My hobbies and how they align with going to Hoi An...

  1. I like to eat and the food here is so good!!!
  2. I like to shop and there was great deals on beautiful silk and linen pieces.
  3. I like to look at pretty things and Hoi An is so very prettttyyyyyyyY!

Skilled gained from visiting Hoi An:

  • Becoming a little more cultured.
  • Haggling. (used at stalls)
  • I declare myself an athlete because I had to walk miles!!!!
